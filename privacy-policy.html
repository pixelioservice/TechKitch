Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At TechKitch, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by TechKitch and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you visit our store or fill out a form on our contact page, we may collect certain information to provide a better shopping experience:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and mobile number when you submit a message or inquiry.
  • Order & Cart Data: Your cart choices are stored locally via your browser session to ensure your selected gear is saved.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our store setup.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our store experience.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our product catalog.
  • Communicate with you via email or mobile number to answer support queries and order inquiries.

3. Data Protection

We value your trust. TechKitch does not sell, trade, or rent your personal contact or mobile information to any third-party marketing networks.

4. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to reach out to our team directly through our Contact Page.
