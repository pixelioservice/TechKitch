Returns & Warranty

We want you to love your tech. If something isn't right, we've got you covered.

7-Day Replacement Warranty

All tech products purchased from TechKitch come with a standard 7-Day Replacement Warranty starting from the day of delivery. If your device exhibits any manufacturing defects or operational issues, we will replace it free of charge.

⚠️ Note: This warranty strictly covers functional and factory defects only. Physical damage, water exposure, or burns are not covered.

Conditions for Returns

To qualify for a successful return or replacement, please ensure the following criteria are met:

  • The product must be in its original, undamaged packaging.
  • All included accessories, manuals, box inserts, and free gifts must be returned complete.
  • The serial number or IMEI must match our official sales logs.
  • Proof of purchase (invoice or order confirmation ID) must be provided.
  • ⚠️ An unboxing video (without any cuts) must be provided to claim the warranty.

Refund Process

If a replacement for your exact product is currently out of stock, we will automatically issue a full refund instead.

Once our technical team inspects and approves the returned product, your refund will be processed back to your original payment method (bKash/Nagad/Bank Transfer) within 3-5 working days.

Need to file a claim?

Our support team is ready to help you sort it out quickly. Contact us with your Order ID and a short video clip of the defect.
